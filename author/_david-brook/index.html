David Brook

I am an experienced End User Computing Engineer who works on delivering Modern Desktop concepts using Microsoft Intune and Configuration Manager.

I’ve worked in IT for the past 8+ years with over 6 of those been a 3rd Line/Senior Engineer delivering solution to Medium/Large enterprise environments. I have worked on Micro Focus Novell ZENworks with ENGL Imaging, Which offered the traditional ‘Gold Image’ creation with the additional driver packs layered on top. More recently I have worked on a project to move away from ZENworks and traditional imaging techniques to the Modern Desktop approach using Intune and Autopilot with Co-Management functionality (Configuration Manager).

Did I mention I have a serious love for Powershell… Nope… Well I do. I have a passion for automation and delivering high standards of work.

I started this blog to share my experiences and also act as a personal Wiki of How-to’s and also share some thinds that I have develop.
